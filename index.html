Divya Varma Chikitsai Maiyam (திவ்யா வர்ம சிகிச்சை மையம்) & Divya Allied Health Science And Technology in Sivakasi is a premier holistic healthcare center led by S. Shanthalakshmi. Our clinic specializes in Varma Therapy, Panchakarma, Acupuncture, and Cupping Therapy — combining ancient Siddha wisdom with modern natural therapeutics.

Located at 70, Gnanagiri Road, Near Coronation Girls School, Sivakasi, we provide personalized, 100% natural, drug-free healing for chronic back pain, joint disorders, paralysis, sciatica, and nerve issues.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, natural, and effective healing through traditional Varma & Ayurvedic therapies in Sivakasi.

Our Vision

To empower patients with natural wellness and train aspiring practitioners through Divya Allied Health Science & Technology.
